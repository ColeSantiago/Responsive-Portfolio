About Me

My name is Cole Santiago. I am 25 years old and I am from Bayonne, New Jersey. I graduated from the Art Institute of New York with an Associate's Degree in Digital Filmmaking. I was a manager at AMC Theatres for over three and a half years. I have a strong intrest in web development and I am currently a student in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp program.

Besides coding, my other hobbies include Science Fiction, Anime, and video games. In my free time I enjoy going to conventions and playing board games.
